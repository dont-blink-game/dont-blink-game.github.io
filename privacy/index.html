Privacy Policy

DON'T BLINK (Studio Scale)

Last updated: December 18, 2025

Summary

DON'T BLINK is a browser game hosted at dont-blink-game.github.io. The blinking version of the game uses your device camera to detect blinking. Camera processing happens locally on your device; we do not upload your camera video.

Camera access and use

  • Why we request camera permission: to detect blinking so the blinking version of the game can function.
  • Required for gameplay: the blinking version requires camera access to play (and is highly recommended).
  • Local processing: blink detection is processed on-device in your browser using Google ML Kit (or equivalent on-device models).
  • No video upload: we do not transmit your camera stream or frames to our servers for storage or analysis.

Your browser controls camera permission. You can deny or revoke camera access at any time in your browser settings. If you do, the blinking version may not work.

Data we store on your device

We store a JSON save file in your browser (for example via local storage) to remember game state and settings. This data stays on your device unless you clear your browser storage.

Data we do not collect (currently)

We do not currently collect analytics, create user accounts, or require you to provide personal information (such as name, email, or phone number) to use the game.

Third-party services

The site is hosted using GitHub Pages. GitHub and other network providers may process basic request data (such as IP address) to deliver the site.

The game may load third-party libraries required for on-device blink detection (for example, Google ML Kit or model assets). When used, those libraries run locally in your browser for gameplay.

Children’s privacy

DON'T BLINK is intended for users aged 13+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Content notice

The game may contain fantasy violence.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the game evolves (for example, if we add analytics or online features). When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact Studio Scale at studioscalegames@gmail.com.
