Privacy Policy

DON'T BLINK (Studio Scale)

Last updated: December 18, 2025

Summary

DON'T BLINK is an Android game developed by Studio Scale. This privacy policy explains how the app uses your device camera for blink detection and what information the app does (and does not) collect.

Camera access and use

  • Why we request camera permission: to detect blinking so the blinking version of the game can function.
  • Required for gameplay: the blinking version requires camera access to play (and is highly recommended).
  • Local processing: blink detection is processed on-device in the app using Google ML Kit (on-device models).
  • No video upload: we do not transmit your camera stream or frames to our servers for storage or analysis.

Android controls camera permission. You can deny or revoke camera access at any time in your device settings. If you do, the blinking version will not work.

Information stored on your device

The app may store game progress and settings locally on your device (in app storage). This information is used to make the game work as expected and is not sent to Studio Scale.

Data we do not collect (currently)

We do not currently collect analytics, create user accounts, or require you to provide personal information (such as name, email, or phone number) to use the game.

Third-party services

The app uses Google ML Kit for on-device blink detection. We do not send your camera stream to Google for processing.

If you download the app via Google Play, Google may collect and process information as part of providing the store and distribution services, in accordance with Google’s policies.

Children’s privacy

DON'T BLINK is intended for users aged 13+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Content notice

The game may contain fantasy violence.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the game evolves (for example, if we add analytics or online features). When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact Studio Scale at studioscalegames@gmail.com.
